About e-Kwivuza

e-Kwivuza is a digital telehealth platform dedicated to providing remote healthcare services to communities in developing regions. Our mission is to make quality healthcare accessible, affordable, and convenient for everyone.

We provide online consultations, AI-powered symptom checking, medicine delivery, and health facility guidance. Our team consists of certified doctors, pharmacists, and health experts committed to improving health outcomes.

Our platform is mobile-first, lightweight, and optimized for low-bandwidth environments, ensuring that anyone can access medical care anytime, anywhere.
